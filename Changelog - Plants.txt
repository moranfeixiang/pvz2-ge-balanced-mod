====== NERFS ======
All Sun Producers:
- Production Time: 23.5 - 25 > 28.5 - 30
- Sun is incredibly to acquire in PvZ2, and the nerf makes it a bit more challenging to plant heavier plants.

Explosive instant kills (Cherry Bomb, Jalapeno, Grapeshot):
- Recharge: 35 > 50
- While it is true that PvZ 2 has a much faster pace than the first game, the recharge of these instas are simply too fast. This nerf should make players more careful when handling explosives.

Grapeshot:
- Damage: 1800 > 600
- Cost: 150 > 175
- Grapeshot deserves a massive nerf to be more comparable to Cherry Bomb. While Grape deals significantly less damage against large groups, it is more potent at taking out weaker zombies in a larger area.

Blover:
- Cost: 50 > 100
- Recharge: 5 > 25
- Blover is a boring plant. Put one down to get rid of all flying zombies for a cheap cheap cost of 50/5. The nerfs require players to think a bit more before using one.

Ice-shroom:
- Freeze Duration: 5 > 7.5
- Chill Duration: 5 > 15
- Rest time: 25 > 45
- Cost: 300 > 425
- Recharge: 30 > 45
- Toughness: 700 > 300
- Being able to freeze an entire lawn is an insanely powerful ability for a plant. Having two Ice-shroom is enough to significantly slow zombie progression, and three can keep the horde frozen indefinitely as long as you can cycle between them.

Doom-shroom:
- Damage: 1800 > 2700
- Burnt Tile Duration: 180 sec > 120 sec
- Cost: 225 > 275
- Recharge: 35 > 75
- The old recharge and cost were simply too cheap for a full-board nuke.

Explode-o-nut:
- Damage: 1800 > 450 (ignores armor)

- Cost: 50 > 150
- Recharge: 10 > 20
- It unbelievable how absurdly overtuned vanilla Explode-o-nut was. The nerfed version can still kill most basic zombies, but will only deal a tiny amount of damage to naturally bulky ones (Mechs, Bullies, Gargantuars).

Primal Wall-nut:
- Cost: 75 > 125
- Recharge: 5 > 7.5
- A Wall-nut that only needs 5 seconds to recharge and can tank 3 Gargantuar smashes is quite crazy. This nerf keeps the plant strong within a reasonable limit.

Cactus:
- Cost: 175 > 225
- Plant Food Pierce Increase: +1 > +2
- Cactus simply outclasses Bloomerang in most situations. Now with the her slight nerf, hopefully Bloomerang will see more use with his higher pierce count.

Sap-fling:
- Cost: 75 > 175
- Recharge: 5 > 15

Homing Thistle:
- Fire Rate: 1.35 > 1.85
- Recharge: 5 > 7.5
